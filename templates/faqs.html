Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Personal Finance Manager?

The Personal Finance Manager is an intuitive tool designed to help you track your expenses and income, set and manage budgets, and monitor your financial health with ease.

How do I add a new transaction?

To add a new transaction, go to the homepage and enter the description, amount, and type of the transaction. Once submitted, it will automatically appear in the transactions table.

Where can I view my transaction history?

Your transaction history is displayed on the transactions page. Here, you can see a detailed table of all your past transactions, sorted by date.

How can I see my total income and expenses?

Head to the reports section. This page provides a comprehensive overview of your financial status, including total income, total expenses, and your net balance.

Can I set a monthly budget?

Yes, you can! Navigate to the budgeting section to set your desired budget for the month. You can specify different budget categories and monitor them closely in the budget table.

What happens when I exceed my budget?

If you exceed your budget, the budgeting section will highlight the overage, allowing you to see where adjustments may be needed.

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. We prioritize your privacy and security, ensuring that your financial data is protected and accessible only to you.

How do I reset all my transactions?

On the transactions page, there is a "Reset" button. Clicking this will delete all your current transactions, allowing you to start fresh.

Can I use the Personal Finance Manager on mobile devices?

Yes, the Personal Finance Manager is designed to be responsive and works seamlessly on both desktop and mobile devices.

Who can I contact for support?

If you have any questions or need assistance, please reach out to our support team via the contact form on our website.
